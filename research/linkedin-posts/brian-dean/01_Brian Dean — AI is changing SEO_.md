## Content (cleaned)

Most people are missing how AI is ACTUALLY changing SEO.

Old SEO focused on:
- On-page optimization
- Keyword targeting
- Ranking #1 for specific keywords

These still matter, but they are no longer enough.

Because:
- AI answers now appear above traditional search results
- Zero-click search is becoming the default
- Rankings no longer guarantee traffic

SEO is shifting from “ranking optimization” to “attention optimization”.

It is becoming closer to social media:
→ focus is now on getting visibility and attention
→ not just clicks from Google rankings

---

## Key Insight

SEO is no longer just about ranking in Google.
It is about being visible inside AI-driven search experiences.

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- AI Overviews reduce dependency on organic clicks
- Visibility matters more than position
- Content must be optimized for AI summarization, not just SERPs

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Build content that AI systems can easily interpret
- Focus on brand + authority signals
- Optimize for multi-surface discovery (Google + AI + platforms)

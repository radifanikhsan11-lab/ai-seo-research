## Content (cleaned)

A new SEO strategy is emerging: LLM Seeding.

The idea is simple:
Instead of only optimizing for Google rankings, you optimize to be **mentioned inside AI-generated answers**.

Traditional SEO logic:
- Rank in Google
- Get clicks
- Drive traffic

New AI-driven logic:
- Be included in AI responses
- Become a trusted citation source
- Influence how AI systems construct answers

In this model:
- Visibility ≠ ranking position
- Visibility = being structurally necessary to the answer

If your brand can be removed without breaking the AI-generated answer, you are not truly visible.

---

## Key Insight

SEO is shifting from “ranking pages” to “becoming a citation source inside AI systems.”

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- LLMs don’t rank websites, they assemble answers
- Only structurally relevant entities get cited
- Brand authority now depends on being part of AI knowledge construction

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Create content with unique frameworks and definitions
- Publish data or insights AI systems can reference
- Build entity-level authority, not just keyword rankings
- Optimize for “being cited” instead of “being clicked”

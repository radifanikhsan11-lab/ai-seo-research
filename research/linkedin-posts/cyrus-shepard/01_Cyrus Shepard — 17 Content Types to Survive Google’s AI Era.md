## Content (cleaned)

Google is entering a zero-click era.

More users are getting answers directly from:
- AI Overviews
- featured snippets
- SERP features

without ever clicking a website.

This means traditional blog content is losing effectiveness.

---

## Core Problem

If AI can answer your content instantly,
users no longer need to visit your site.

Generic informational content is the most at risk.

---

## Key Insight

Not all content survives in the AI era.

Only content types that:
- provide unique value
- contain proprietary information
- cannot be easily replicated by AI

will continue to perform.

---

## High-Survival Content Types (Examples)

- Original research & data
- Case studies
- Expert interviews
- Proprietary frameworks
- Tools / calculators
- Community-driven insights

---

## Low-Survival Content Types

- Generic blog posts
- Rewritten content
- “Top 10” listicles without unique value
- AI-generated summaries of existing info

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- AI reduces demand for generic informational content
- Content must provide “information gain” beyond what AI can generate
- SEO shifts from content volume → content uniqueness

---

## Strategic Shift

- From publishing more content → to publishing better, unique content
- From keyword targeting → to proprietary insight creation
- From traffic focus → to differentiation and authority

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Invest in original data and research
- Create content AI cannot replicate
- Build expert-driven or experience-based content
- Eliminate low-value, generic articles

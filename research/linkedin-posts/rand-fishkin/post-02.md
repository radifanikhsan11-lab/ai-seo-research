## Content (cleaned)

Is modern SEO (AI Overviews, LLM visibility, brand mentions) harder than classic SEO?

Rand’s answer:

→ It’s not harder  
→ It’s different  

---

## Old SEO (2015 Era)

- Keyword optimization  
- Link building  
- Anchor text manipulation  
- Ranking pages in Google  

Success depended heavily on:
→ backlinks  
→ technical optimization  
→ keyword targeting  

---

## New SEO (AI Era)

- Appearing in AI answers (LLMs, AI Overviews)  
- Being cited across platforms  
- Building brand presence in trusted sources  
- Influencing what gets mentioned  

---

## Key Insight

SEO has shifted from:

→ ranking pages  

to:

→ earning mentions in the right places  

---

## Core Difference

Old SEO:
- mechanical  
- system-focused  
- optimization-heavy  

New SEO:
- creative  
- brand-driven  
- influence-based  

---

## Supporting Idea

Rand emphasizes:

Creating things people want to:
- talk about  
- share  
- reference  

is now more valuable than optimizing pages.

This includes:
- original research  
- data studies  
- unique insights  

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- AI systems pull from trusted, widely-referenced sources  
- Mentions and citations matter more than backlinks  
- Content must be *reference-worthy*, not just optimized  
- Distribution becomes as important as creation  

---

## Strategic Shift

- From SEO → to influence engineering  
- From backlinks → to brand mentions  
- From ranking → to being cited  

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Create content worth referencing (data, research, insights)  
- Focus on distribution (media, communities, platforms)  
- Build brand authority across the web  
- Think beyond Google rankings

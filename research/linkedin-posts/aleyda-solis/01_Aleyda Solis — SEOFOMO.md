## Content (cleaned)

SEO is evolving faster than ever due to AI-driven search changes.

A major shift happening now is that SEO is no longer only about ranking in Google.

Instead, search visibility is expanding across:
- Google Search
- AI Overviews
- LLM-based answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)

This creates a new challenge for SEO professionals:
👉 How do we optimize content for systems that summarize, not just rank?

Traditional SEO focused on:
- keywords
- backlinks
- on-page optimization

But AI search introduces new requirements:
- content must be structured for extraction
- information must be clear and machine-readable
- authority signals matter more than ever

SEOFOMO highlights that marketers need to rethink SEO as a **multi-system visibility strategy**, not just a Google ranking strategy.

---

## Key Insight

SEO is shifting from “search engine optimization” to “search ecosystem optimization.”

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- Search is no longer a single platform (Google only)
- AI systems act as intermediaries between users and content
- Content must be optimized for both ranking AND AI summarization
- Visibility depends on being included in AI-generated answers

---

## Strategic Shift

- From Google-first SEO → to multi-surface search visibility
- From keyword targeting → to entity + topic authority
- From ranking pages → to being referenced in AI outputs

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Optimize content for AI readability (clear structure, definitions, entities)
- Strengthen topical authority across clusters, not single keywords
- Build content that AI systems can safely summarize and cite

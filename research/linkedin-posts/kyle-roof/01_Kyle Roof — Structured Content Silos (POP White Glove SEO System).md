## Content (cleaned)

Not all SEO content is created equally.

Inside structured SEO systems (like POP White Glove), content is not published randomly.

Instead, it is built using a **content silo architecture**, where:

- Top-level pages target main topics
- Supporting content expands subtopics
- Internal linking connects all pieces into a unified structure

This creates a tightly connected topical system.

---

## Core Concept

SEO performance improves when content is organized into:

→ hierarchical topic clusters (content silos)

instead of isolated blog posts.

---

## Key Insight

Google evaluates **topical relevance and structure**, not just individual pages.

A well-organized content system:
- distributes authority across pages
- strengthens relevance signals
- improves ranking stability

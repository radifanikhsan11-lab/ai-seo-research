## Content (cleaned)

Most keyword tools focus on:

- search volume  
- competition level  

But they miss a critical factor:

👉 whether your site can actually rank for that keyword right now

---

## Core Problem

Many SEO strategies fail because:

- teams target high-volume “dream keywords”  
- content is built without considering site authority  
- results stagnate despite consistent publishing  

---

## Key Insight

The fastest SEO growth comes from:

👉 targeting keywords that match your current site strength  

NOT the biggest keywords available  

---

## POP Impact Score Concept

Kyle introduces an internal framework:

POP Impact Score measures:

- current site authority (how Google sees your site)  
- realistic ranking opportunities  
- optimal keyword sequencing  

This enables:

→ predictable, compounding SEO growth

- AI-driven SEO systems require realistic targeting, not guesswork

## Strategic Shift

- From volume-based keyword selection → to feasibility-based targeting  
- From “big wins” mindset → to compounding growth strategy  
- From guessing → to measurable SEO systems  

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Audit your current site authority before choosing keywords  
- Start with “winnable” keywords to build momentum  
- Gradually move to more competitive keywords  
- Treat SEO as a progression system, not a shortcut

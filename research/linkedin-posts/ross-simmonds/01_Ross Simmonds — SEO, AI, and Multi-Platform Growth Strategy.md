## Content (cleaned)

Modern SEO is no longer limited to Google rankings.

Brands now need to show up wherever users search for answers, including:
- Google Search
- YouTube
- Reddit
- AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)

The key shift is not about naming conventions like:
SEO, GEO, AEO, LLMO — the labels don’t matter.

What matters is:
👉 Can your brand be discovered across all discovery systems?

---

## Core Insight

The future of search is multi-platform and experimentation-driven.

There is no single “correct” SEO strategy anymore.

Winning strategies include:
- long-form keyword targeting
- YouTube content for citation influence
- Reddit participation for community visibility
- content distribution across multiple channels

---

## AI / SEO Implication

- AI systems pull information from multiple sources, not just websites
- Content visibility depends on distribution, not just optimization
- Authority is built across platforms, not in one channel
- SEO is merging with content marketing + distribution strategy

---

## Strategic Shift

- From SEO as a Google-only system → to distributed discovery system
- From ranking optimization → to presence optimization
- From static strategy → to continuous experimentation

---

## Actionable Takeaway

- Publish content across multiple platforms (not just blogs)
- Invest in YouTube and Reddit for citation influence
- Treat SEO as part of a broader distribution strategy
- Focus on experimentation instead of fixed “best practices”
